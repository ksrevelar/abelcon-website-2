Company Background

ABELCON Builders Corporation was established on July 29, 2015, by Engr. Joseph Abello, initially as a single proprietorship named BBNA Construction. It transitioned into a newly established Corporation on April 29, 2021.

Starting with small-scale jobs like painting, masonry, and civil works, the company grew to become an accredited contractor for major partners like Security Bank Corporation and Star Appliance Centre Inc. We have achieved remarkable success in renovating numerous Security Bank branches and schools donated by the Security Bank Foundation nationwide.

Abelcrete®
Walling System

We are the proud innovators of ABELCRETE®, a proprietary wall system designed for speed, strength, and efficiency.

PATENTED SYSTEM

What is Abelcrete®?

A wall system that sprays concrete with high velocity using a wet mixture composed of cement, sand, and water onto installed rebars with mesh.

Why Choose Abelcrete?

Key Advantages

Expedited Construction

Computed to be 40% faster than traditional CHB layout. Less idle time, no plastering, and no chipping required.

Superb Strength

Pressurized nozzle application creates high strength, low permeability, and high durability walls.

Fire Resistant

Can withstand up to 4 hours of direct exposure and is not flammable up to 980°C.

Excellent Sound Absorption

Sound transmission class (STC) of min 42 dB / max 47 dB. Ideal for structures requiring acoustic insulation.

High Versatility & Design Flexibility

Extremely adaptable for high walls, domes, and architecturally curved finished surfaces. Suitable for complex geometric designs.

Press Release

Abelcon Completes Quezon City Skyline Mixed-Use Tower Ahead of Schedule

November 10, 2025 • Quezon City

Abelcon Builders Corporation is proud to announce the successful turnover of the Skyline Mixed-Use Tower, a 35-story development located in the heart of Diliman, Quezon City.

The project, valued at ₱450 Million, was completed two weeks ahead of the projected timeline, showcasing Abelcon's commitment to efficiency and project management excellence.

Engineering Excellence

The Skyline Tower presented unique challenges due to its location in a high-density area. To mitigate traffic impact and accelerate construction, Abelcon utilized advanced pre-cast concrete methodologies for the building's facade and structural walls. This approach reduced on-site pouring time by 30%.

"Our team worked in synchronized 24/7 shifts to ensure continuous progress," said Engr. Juan Dela Cruz, Project Manager. "The use of BIM (Building Information Modeling) allowed us to foresee structural clashes before they happened, saving us weeks of potential rework."

Sustainable Features

Aligning with Abelcon's mission for sustainability, the tower includes:

  • Rainwater Harvesting System: Collects water for landscape irrigation and maintenance.
  • Solar-Ready Roof Deck: Pre-installed infrastructure for future solar panel integration.
  • Low-E Glass Facade: Reduces heat gain, lowering energy consumption for air conditioning.

"This project sets a new benchmark for mixed-use developments in Metro Manila. It stands as a testament to what Filipino engineering can achieve."

— CEO, Abelcon Builders Corp.

The Skyline Mixed-Use Tower is now open for commercial leasing and residential occupancy.

Abelcon Awarded Top Safety Contractor by DOLE

October 24, 2025 • Manila

We are honored to receive the prestigious "Safe Work Award" from the Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE) for our outstanding safety performance in 2025.

This recognition underscores Abelcon's unyielding commitment to the well-being of our workforce. Across 15 active project sites in Metro Manila and Central Luzon, Abelcon achieved a milestone of 2 Million Safe Man-Hours without a lost-time injury (LTI).

Our Safety Department has implemented a rigorous "Zero Accident" program that includes:

  • Strict PPE compliance and regular equipment audits.

"Safety is not just a policy; it's our culture," stated Engr. Maria Santos, Head of Safety. "Every worker deserves to go home to their family safe and sound. This award belongs to every carpenter, mason, and engineer who looks out for one another."

We dedicate this award to our hardworking teams on the ground. Abelcon will continue to set the standard for occupational safety in the construction industry.

Project Update

New Logistics Hub Breaks Ground in Bulacan

September 15, 2025 • Bulacan

Abelcon Builders Corporation has officially broken ground on the new "North Point Logistics Center" in Plaridel, Bulacan, marking our expansion into large-scale industrial infrastructure.

The 5-hectare development aims to support the booming e-commerce sector in the Philippines. Once completed, the facility will feature 30,000 square meters of warehouse space, cold storage capabilities, and an automated sorting center.

Strategic Location

Situated near the Plaridel Bypass Road, the hub offers strategic access to both Metro Manila and Northern Luzon. This project is expected to generate over 500 jobs during the construction phase alone.

"Industrial construction requires speed and precision," explained Engr. Pedro Reyes. "We are utilizing pre-engineered steel structures (PEB) to expedite the structural frame erection. We aim to have the first phase operational by Q2 2026."

"This project signifies Abelcon's readiness to tackle complex industrial requirements. We are building the backbone of the country's supply chain."

Updates on the construction progress will be posted monthly on our website.

Company News

PCAB License Upgraded to Category B

August 01, 2025 • Corporate

Abelcon Builders Corporation is pleased to announce that the Philippine Contractors Accreditation Board (PCAB) has upgraded our license to **Category B** for General Building.

This significant milestone reflects our company's financial stability, technical competence, and track record of successful project delivery. The upgrade allows Abelcon to undertake larger contracts and participate in more complex government and private bidding processes.

What This Means for Clients

With a Category B license, Abelcon now has increased Allowable Ranges for Contract Costs (ARCC). This enables us to:

  • Bid for multi-story building projects with higher valuations.
  • Take on larger-scale government infrastructure contracts.
  • Partner with international firms for joint venture projects.

"This upgrade is a testament to our team's hard work over the past four years," said the CEO. "We started as a small specialty contractor, and today we stand ready to build the nation's most critical structures."

We thank our clients, partners, and employees for their continued trust and support as we reach new heights.
